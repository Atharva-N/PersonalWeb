Countries Chain

The "Countries Chain" app is a word chain game where players take turns naming countries that begin with the last letter of the previously named country. The game can be played either against the computer or in a multiplayer mode. In the single-player mode, players compete against the computer to see how many consecutive correct country names they can provide before time runs out. In the multiplayer mode, players take turns naming countries until one player is unable to come up with a valid country name. The app features a timer to add an element of urgency to the game and keeps track of the player's score or streak. Overall, the app provides a fun and challenging way to test your knowledge of countries while improving your vocabulary and thinking skills.
